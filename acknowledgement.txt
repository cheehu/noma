3. django-inline-admin-extensions
Django admin interface page with tabularinline will become slow to load when the list of tabular iems become too long (> 30 items). 
The solution is to add pagination (with 20 items per page) to the tabularinline. After googling, I found a working solution from:
https://github.com/ctxis/django-inline-admin-extensions
